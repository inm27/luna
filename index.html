Hospital Charter

Patient Rights & Responsibilities

We are committed to upholding the following rights and responsibilities for every patient in our care.

Patient Rights

  1. Right to be treated with dignity, courtesy, and respect with no discrimination.
  2. Right to information on your health condition, diagnosis, treatment and expected outcome.
  3. Right to information on expected turnaround time for different services.
  4. Right to consent to or refuse a treatment procedure.
  5. Right to seek a second medical opinion.
  6. Right to protection, confidentiality and privacy.

Patient Responsibilities

  1. To treat hospital staff and other clients with respect and dignity.
  2. Provide relevant information to health care providers to guide their treatment plan.
  3. Seek information and clarifications from health care providers regarding their treatment.
  4. Comply with treatment and medical instructions and adhere to follow-up appointments.
  5. Timely payment for all services received.
  6. Maintaining a safe and hygienic environment and protecting the property of the health facility.
